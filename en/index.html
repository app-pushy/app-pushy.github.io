FAQ

Common questions

Does the camera record or store video?
No. Camera input is processed in real time on your device only. Nothing is saved, transmitted, or shared.
Push-ups aren't being counted.
Place your phone on the floor facing up so the front camera sees your face. Best results at 30–50 cm distance. Low lighting can reduce accuracy.
I can't hear the coach.
Check that your silent switch is off and volume is turned up. The app uses the main speaker output.
What does premium include?
Removes ads and unlocks 6 additional coaches beyond the free Drill Sergeant and Late-Night DJ.
Can't restore my purchase.
Go to Settings → Restore Purchase inside the app. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
