Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I denied camera permission.
Go to Settings → Pushy → Camera and set it to Allow.

Q. Push-ups aren't being counted automatically.
Place your phone face-up on the floor and lie down facing the front camera. Best results with your face 30–50 cm from the screen. Low light can reduce accuracy.

Q. Can't restore my premium purchase.
Go to Settings → Restore Purchase inside the app. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
