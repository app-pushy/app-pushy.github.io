Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously.

Pushy does not collect or store any personal information.

Information We Collect

Pushy does not collect any personal data or user information. All data — including workout history and settings — is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Camera and Face Data

The front camera is used solely to detect and count push-up repetitions. Camera input is processed in real time on your device only — it is never recorded, saved, or transmitted.

The app detects facial position and movement through the front camera to count reps. This data is processed entirely on-device in real time. It is never stored, sent to external servers, or shared with third parties. It is used exclusively for push-up counting.

Photo Library

Photo library access is requested only when you choose to save a share card to your camera roll after completing a workout. The app does not access or collect any existing photos.

Advertising

The free version displays ads via Google AdMob. Ad-related data processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple. Payment information is managed by Apple and is never accessible to Pushy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at app.pushy@gmail.com.
